Kale caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken untill golden brown and 165 in middle.

    Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing untill coated.

    Time: 5 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    Time: 5 minutes
